# Slides — Sales & Marketing Scenario

**Job-to-be-done:** Win the engagement. Build credibility, demonstrate capability, motivate the next conversation.

**Typical deck shape:** Title → about us → services → sector experience → 2–3 case studies → testimonials → ways of working → commercials → contact. ~25–40 slides.

**Density:** Live-presented sparse, read-ahead moderate. Prefer presenter-led.

---

## Do

- Front-load proof: customer logos, sector experience, named case studies before any methodology slides.
- Tailor the deck per opportunity — drop the sectors that don't apply, replace the case studies with ones the prospect's peers would recognise.
- Use testimonial slides as transitions between sections — they pace the read and add third-party voice.
- Close with a named human (Client Principal), photograph, and direct contact route, not a generic "thank you".

---

## Don't

- Do not lead with "About us" for more than two slides — prospects want to know what you'd do for them, not your history.
- Do not include speculative pricing in the deck body — put it in an appendix or a separate commercial document.
- Do not list every client logo you have — curate to the prospect's sector and adjacent sectors.
