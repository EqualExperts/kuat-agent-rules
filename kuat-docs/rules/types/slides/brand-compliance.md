# Slides — Brand Compliance

Logo placement, page-number badge, bracket motif, and co-branding for presentation decks.

**See also:** [foundations/logo.md](../../foundations/logo.md), [foundations/brand.md](../../foundations/brand.md), [styling.md](./styling.md).

---

## Do

- Place the EE wordmark + bracket-E logo on the title slide and the contact/closing slide, bottom-right or in the blue title bar.
- Use the page-number badge (EE Blue square, white numeral, top-right) on all body slides except title, full-bleed image, and section dividers.
- Use the bracket motif as a framing device — typically top-left + bottom-right, sized to anchor a content block or photograph. Keep brackets in EE Blue.
- Keep the deck consistently masterbrand. Sub-brand or co-branded lockups (client + EE) follow the Atlassian-style endorsement pattern from [foundations/brand.md](../../foundations/brand.md).
- Include the EE logo once on the closing slide alongside `equalexperts.com` and social handles.

---

## Don't

- Do not place the EE logo on every slide — the page-number badge is the persistent brand cue, not the logo.
- Do not let the bracket motif and the bracket-E logo touch or overlap on the same slide — they compete visually. If both are needed, separate them with whitespace or move the logo to the footer bar.
- Do not recolour, outline, rotate, or apply effects (drop shadow, glow, bevel) to the logo.
- Do not stretch, crop, or place the logo on a busy photographic area without a solid backing panel.
- Do not co-brand a client logo at equal weight to EE on a sales deck cover — EE leads, the client logo sits in the body as context.
